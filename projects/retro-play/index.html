Retro Play

This software lets you search, download, play and manage a library of classic games. Think of it as an iTunes of old games.

Retro Play

Retro Play offers a simple, clean interface to manage your classic game library.

Search

With just a console and a keyword provided, Retro Play searches through a database of over a thousand games.

Download

When you double-click a result, Retro Play starts downloading it. In a few seconds you'll have it in you library.

Manage

In the library, you can manage your games and savefiles. And of course, play your games with a double click.

Play

Relive your childhood games, or discover hidden treasures you've never played.
